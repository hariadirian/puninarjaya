Our Partnership

Kerry Logistics

Kerry Logistics Network Limited a leading logistics service provider in Asia, has a far-reaching global network that stretches across six continents, and includes the largest distribution network and hub operations in Greater China and the ASEAN region.

Kerry Logistics Network Limited is principally engaged in the integrated logistics and international freight forwarding businesses. The Company has more than 400 service locations in 35 countries and territories across Asia, Australia, Europe, and North and South America. It provides integrated logistics services, as a third-party logistics service provider, for manufacturers, retailers and other customers worldwide. Its integrated logistics business comprises logistics operations, which involve the provision of a wide range of logistics services, such as storage and value-added services, trucking and distribution, returns management and various ancillary services, primarily in Asia, and Hong Kong warehouse, which involves the leasing of warehousing space in Hong Kong to customers. It also provides the majority of its international freight forwarding services intra-Asia and between Asia and Europe.

Combination between Kerry Logistics’ global logistics expertise and international freight forwarding network with Puninar Logistics’ local capabilities and connections, will give customers in Indonesia access to complete supply chain solutions and a globally integrated network.

KEPPEL LOGISTICS

Keppel Logistics is a mainboard-listed company in Singapore with core businesses in logistics and data centres and operations in Asia Pacific and Europe. Keppel Logistics operates world-class logistics facilities with state-of-the-art IT infrastructure in China, Hong Kong, Malaysia, Indonesia, Vietnam, and Australia.

Headquartered in Singapore, Keppel Logistics is one of the country's founding supply chain & logistics service providers with nearly 50 years of experience. It has more than three million square feet of warehousing facilities to support comprehensive and integrated end-to-end logistical services in warehousing, transportation, distribution, value-add projects, freight management and consolidation services. Keppel Logistics takes pride in its commitment to reliability and flexibility for its customers

MITSUI-SOKO EXPRESS

As part of the Mitsui-Soko and Toyota groups, Mitsui-Soko Express is a global express forwarder, expertise in procurement, production and sales logistics. Deep experience with specialized know-how for handling auto parts, chemicals, and dangerous goods, Mitsui-Soko Express continuously strive for swift, safe, and reliable transport routes to match the characteristics of each cargo and demands by combining methods such as third party logistics, combined transport, and modes.

Mitsui-Soko Express go further to minimize transportation costs. In addition, to achieve high-quality logistics, has expanded to utilize IT to maximize efficiency of its logistics, as well as inventory management of imported cargo and distribution processing.
